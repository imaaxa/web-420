What is application state?

"Application State (also known as Program State) represents the totality of everything necessary to keep your application running. When we refer to application state we are normally referring to the state of the program as it exists in the contents of its memory. (Quisenberry, 2018)"

Once the requested page is loaded, that is the state of the application. The browser has everything needed to display to the user it's representation of what was requested.

What is resource state?

"Resource State refers to the state of the resources being stored on the server. (Quisenberry, 2018)"
